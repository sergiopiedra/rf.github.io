BE THE CHANGE: WORLD WATER DAY

  • By
  • John Stauch, serves as Pentair President and Chief Executive Officer
  • Mar 22, 2023

Water flows through everything we do, from the water we drink in our homes, to the infrastructure that shapes our communities, to the industries that drive our economy and the resources that sustain our planet.

That’s why today, and every day, we celebrate World Water Day, designated by the United Nations to raise awareness of the world’s water and sanitation crisis. I am particularly inspired by this year’s theme of “Be the Change,” which encourages people to take action in their own lives to change the way they use, consume and manage water.

At Pentair, we take seriously our responsibility to provide more effortless solutions to help address the global water challenges of the future. Driven by our Purpose of creating a better world for people and the planet through smart, sustainable water solutions, we are focused on helping the world sustainably move, improve and enjoy water, life's most essential resource.

MOVE WATER

Whether it’s moving water throughout the home with our residential pumps or our highly engineered flood control solutions that help protect cities during times of inclement weather, our solutions deliver water where it’s needed, when it’s needed, more efficiently. As water resources become increasingly scarce, our energy-efficient pumping solutions help reduce costs while improving the efficiency of transporting water from source to treatment facility, and ultimately, to the end user.

IMPROVE WATER

Clean, reliable water sustains people and the planet, and our commercial and residential water solutions provide great tasting, higher-quality water and ice while helping people use water more productively. In addition, our solutions provide benefits such as softened water to help prevent scale which extends the life of water-using equipment and filtration that reduces the need for single-use plastic water bottles.

MOVE WATER

Our innovative, energy efficient pool solutions help people more sustainably enjoy water. For instance, Pentair was the first pool equipment manufacturer to have an ENERGY STAR®-certified pool pump, and has been recognized as an ENERGY STAR Partner of the Year since 2013. In 2022, our ENERGY STAR-certified pool pumps helped U.S. consumers save approximately 1 billion kWh of energy, resulting in GHG emissions reductions of 414,974 tons of CO2 and saving $142 million in operating costs.*

Water is a basic, universal human need. It is essential to our lives and the planet, and it’s essential to every single one of us. That’s why I am proud of the work we do at Pentair to help the world sustainably move, improve and enjoy water. Every day.

* Savings are based on a weighted average annual kWh savings from the Consortium for Energy Efficiency (CEE) at $0.14 / kWh. Individual Weighted Energy Factor (WEF) scores and savings may vary by model. For more information regarding the energy efficiency features of current Pentair pool pump offerings, including WEF scores, please visit Pentair.com/energystar.
