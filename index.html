RF Engineers is a company dedicated to consulting, sales, installation and maintenance of hydraulic equipment and systems, such as: pumping systems, irrigation systems, swimming pool equipment, solar heaters, wastewater treatment systems, water purification systems and water softeners, as well as turnkey projects such as aqueducts or water distribution systems and fire fighting systems.

Our Services

Pump equipment

The best brands and support of water pumps for constant pressure systems, hydropneumatic systems, irrigation systems, well equipment, sewage, among others.

Water purification equipment

Clean water for your project, equipment to eliminate bacteria, bad odors, metals, calcium, turbidity in the water, among others.

Pool equipment

Residential and commercial filtration systems, automation systems, energy saving, as well as pool accessories, chemicals and minerals.
